Securely control and monitor your garage door from anywhere.

Smart Garage Control for Homey

Take full control of your garage door with this high-performance, low-latency driver. Whether you are arriving home or checking your home security at night, this app integrates your garage door opener seamlessly into the Homey ecosystem.

Key Features:
- Real-Time Status: Instantly see if your garage is Open or Closed via the integrated door position feedback.
- Secure Flows: Use your garage door as a trigger or an action in Homey Flows. For example, "When the last person leaves home, close the garage door."
- Native Integration: Designed specifically for the 'garagedoor' class in Homey for a natural look and feel in the mobile app.

Supported Devices:
This app is specifically optimized for Tuya-based Zigbee Garage Door Openers (Model:TS0601). 

Note: This driver is designed for devices that use a wired or included wireless door position sensor to ensure the status in Homey is always accurate.

Installation & Setup:
1. Install this app on your Homey Pro.
2. Put your Garage Door Opener into pairing mode.
3. Add the device through the "Add Device" wizard by selecting "Smart Garage Control".
